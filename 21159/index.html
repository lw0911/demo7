Seminars and internships

  • Business seminars

    ConfCentre conducts free point and offsite (1-2 working days) business seminars, the geography of which covers almost the whole world, for specialists, managers, businessmen and entrepreneurs who want to get acquainted with the advanced experience of Japan in solving a wide range of issues. The seminars have a wide range of topics - from the discussion of general issues to the study of specific industries for specialists ("Urban infrastructure in harsh climatic conditions", "Increasing tourist attractiveness", etc.). Seminars are read by specialists, consultants or entrepreneurs with extensive experience in the Asia-Pacific region, the USA, and Europe.

  • Internships

    ConfCentre offers graduate internships on a wide range of topics. The duration of the internship programs is 10 days, which include a short theoretical part, and primarily focus on visits and inspections of enterprises, meetings with Japanese businessmen. Each branch has a fixed number of internship participants that it can select.

* Applicants are considered who did not complete the Presidential program, or who have completed, but have already used their right to a foreign internship.
